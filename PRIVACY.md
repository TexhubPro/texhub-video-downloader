# Privacy Policy — TexHub Video Downloader

_Last updated: 2026-06-05_

**TexHub Video Downloader** is built privacy-first. This policy explains exactly what
the extension does — and does not — do with your data.

## Summary

> **The extension collects nothing.** No personal data, no browsing history, no
> analytics. Nothing is sent to us or any third party.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** TexHub Video Downloader does not collect, store on a remote server,
transmit, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or usage analytics. There is no
account, no tracking, and no telemetry.

## How it works

- Stream detection, playlist parsing, preview, downloading and MP4 conversion all run
  **locally in your browser**.
- Network requests are made **only** to the video servers required to detect or
  download the media you explicitly choose, using your existing browser session and
  credentials (the same ones the website already uses).
- The list of detected sources and in-progress downloads is kept in the browser's
  local/session storage on your own device and is cleared automatically when a download
  finishes or the tab is closed. **It never leaves your device.**

## Permissions

The host and API permissions requested by the extension are used solely to detect,
preview and download media from the page you are viewing. They are never used to track
you. See the [README](README.md) for a per-permission explanation.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published with the extension
and the “Last updated” date above will change.
